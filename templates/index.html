Kapila Bakers

Kapila Mapa who is the owner of the Kapila bakers. It is a small level shop & bakery with 5 years’ experience. The shop is situated in Kandy town and there are shop owner and 4 permanent employees.
We sell bakery items including bun, pastry, cake etc. Bakery items are prepared by themselves in their bakery by using electric oven & mixing machine-like new technologies. High level of Hugine is maintained because they don’t use the manual mixing method.
ULarge customer base is available. Usually, 300 customers come to their shop. Chun-Pan vehicles cover a 10-15 km area customer base.

Works of the bakery is started at 6.00 a.m. and all employees must report before 6.00a.the work is finished at 9.00 p.m. the time of bakery and shop are closed. hygiene of the product and workers are highly maintained. Monthly salary is given for the employees specific recipes are used to make bakery items. Fully automated system is used to processing bakery items. Flour and other ingredients are taken from one supplier in the town from the point this business started to present.
